About Us

There's this notion that to grow a medical tourism business, you have to be ruthless. But we know there's a better way to grow — one where patients come first and where growing a company means growing people, too. Where eye care decisions are driven by trust, not hidden costs. That's why EyeGo Korea exists.

Our Mission: Helping Patients See Better, Safely

We believe patients should be treated like people, not procedures. That's why EyeGo Korea is committed to creating a patient experience that prioritizes transparency, safety, and genuine care. We do this by partnering only with strictly vetted clinics, providing verified feedback, and standing as your local advocate throughout your journey — from consultation to post-op recovery.

Built on Trust, Verified in Person

Every clinic in our network is personally vetted and routinely re-verified. We conduct regular review audits so you see the most current, honest patient experiences. When you travel to Korea for eye care, EyeGo Korea is your local guarantor — ensuring your voice is heard, your safety is protected, and your healing is supported long after you leave the clinic.
